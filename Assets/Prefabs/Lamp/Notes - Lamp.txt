The animations on the lamp (LampOn and LampOff) do two things.  First, they
set the intensity of the point light source centered on the bulb.  Second,
they set the emission intensity of the shade material.  Be mindful when
altering this material.

The lamp is a modification of the model by M.Zamzam found here:
